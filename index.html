History

The Inspiration

When I was 5 years old I played baseball, and during my games the couch's son would always sit in the dugout waiting for the game to finish. To pass the time he played on his DS, so whenever I was on the bench the only thing I was interested in was the game he was playing, which was always Pokémon Black and White. It drew all of my attention away from the game and disapointed me when I had to leave the bench to play.

The Conection

I grew up watching Pokémon's cartoon series, which probably inspired me to be involved with the franchise as much as I have been. Before school every morning I would turn on the TV to watch an episode of Pokémon Dimond and Pearl. It was something I could always look forward to even if everything was wrong I could sit down to watch Pokémon and it always made me happier.

The Help

Before I can even remember i've had this toolbox filled with Pokémon cards. It was gifted to me and my brother by our cousins who had moved on from the franchise. my brother and I would stay up through the night counting every card in that box. I would watch him play Pokémon Emerald, and whenever he wasn't playing that's how I would spend my time. I was playing the game before I could even read. My favorite thing is that my family never dicouraged me they even once through a Pokémon birthday party for me when I was 8.
